#+TITLE: Nix

[[https://nixos.org/][Nix]] is a powerful package manager for Linux and other Unix systems that makes package
management reliable and reproducible. Share your development and build environments across
different machines.

[[file:nixos.org][NixOS]] is a Linux distribution with a unique approach to package and configuration
management. Built on top of the Nix package manager, it is completely declarative, makes
upgrading systems reliable, and has many other [[https://nixos.org/features.html][advantages]].
